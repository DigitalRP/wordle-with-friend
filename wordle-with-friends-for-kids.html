Parents can employ several strategies to maximize learning during Wordle With Friends sessions. Encourage children to sound out letters and words before making a guess. Encourage your child to say the letters and words aloud. This auditory reinforcement helps them connect sounds with spelling. It reinforces phonetic awareness, which is vital for reading and writing.
